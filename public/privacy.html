Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 13, 2024

Welcome to Deadblock ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our game application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account or use our services, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and profile picture
  • Authentication Data: Information provided through Google Sign-In (email, name, profile picture)
  • Game Data: Game statistics, scores, achievements, and match history

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use Deadblock, we automatically collect:

  • Device information (type, operating system)
  • Usage data (features used, game sessions)
  • Log data (access times, app crashes)

2. Google User Data

Google API Services User Data Policy

Deadblock's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

2.1 Data We Access from Google

When you sign in with Google, we only request access to:

  • Basic Profile Information: Your name, email address, and profile picture
  • Email Address: Used for account identification and communication

2.2 How We Use Google Data

We use Google user data solely to:

  • Create and authenticate your Deadblock account
  • Display your name and profile picture within the game
  • Send important account-related communications

2.3 Limited Use Disclosure

Our use of Google user data is limited to the practices explicitly disclosed in this Privacy Policy. We do not:

  • Sell, rent, or lease your Google user data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Transfer your data for unrelated purposes
  • Use your data in ways that violate Google's policies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our game services
  • Create and manage your account
  • Enable multiplayer features and matchmaking
  • Track game statistics and leaderboards
  • Send notifications about your games (with your permission)
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Other Players: Your username, profile picture, rating, and game statistics are visible to other players
  • Service Providers: We use Supabase for authentication and database services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication through OAuth 2.0
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication requirements

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

8. Children's Privacy

Deadblock is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with:

  • Google Sign-In: For authentication (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Supabase: For backend services (Supabase Privacy Policy)
  • Netlify: For hosting (Netlify Privacy Policy)

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@deadblock.app
