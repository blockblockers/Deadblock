About DEADBLOCK

DEADBLOCK is a modern take on classic pentomino puzzle games, combining strategic depth with competitive multiplayer action. Inspired by games like Blokus and traditional polyomino puzzles, DEADBLOCK offers a unique gaming experience that's easy to learn but challenging to master.

The game features 12 different pentomino pieces - shapes made from 5 connected squares. Players take turns placing pieces on a shared 8√ó8 board, with the goal of controlling as much territory as possible while blocking their opponent's moves.

Whether you're a casual player looking for a quick brain teaser or a competitive gamer seeking ranked matches, DEADBLOCK has something for everyone. The progressive web app technology means you can install it on any device and even play offline.
